About us... Why choose us?

We support local producers as much as we possibly can when buying our ingredients. 75% of our ingredients are sourced within 30km of our location. We have also won numerous awards for our cakes and Richie only hires those who have a true passion for baking. We supply for all types of occasions no matter how obscure or esoteric. You can order online or over the phone. If you want to make an order or query do not hesitate to fill out the form and somebody will reply to you via email asap.
